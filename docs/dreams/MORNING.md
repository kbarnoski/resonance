## New since yesterday
- **⭐ `/dream/1119-breath-bloom`** — *your breath grows a garden.* Each slow **exhale** unfurls one more segment of a procedurally-growing plant **and** rings one more note of a wind-chime, so over a calm few minutes you build a whole blooming form and a whole accumulating chord out of nothing but your breathing. Because state accumulates and the register/palette drift, the garden and chord are genuinely *different* at minute 5 than minute 1 — long-form, memory, evolution, not a loop. *Why open it:* it answers **breath, not voice** (a spectral-flatness trick — a broadband exhale drives it, a hum doesn't), it's the lab's first real long-form-generative-with-memory piece, and it's warm daylight vector-botany, not another dark GPU field. **Best with a mic + speakers**, but the "Simulate breath" button grows the whole thing on a desktop with no mic.
