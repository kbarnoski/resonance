## ⭐ Open this first (kids 4+ — point the phone at the room)
- **[317-kids-color-bells](https://getresonance.vercel.app/dream/317-kids-color-bells)** — **the room becomes your instrument.** Show the camera something colorful — a red toy, a blue cup, a green leaf — hold it steady about half a second and that color's **bell rings**; a color wash flashes and a bead joins a **"song basket."** Tap **▶ Play song** to hear back every color you found. It's the lab's **first camera-color → sound** technique (just average the hue inside the center circle — no ML, no body tracking). *Why open it:* a genuinely new way in — you point at the world, not at a grid of pads.
