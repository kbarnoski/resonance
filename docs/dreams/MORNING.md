## New since yesterday — open this first
- **[/dream/1408-wolf-ring](https://getresonance.vercel.app/dream/1408-wolf-ring)** — *walk the circle of fifths inside a real historical temperament, and play the **wolf**.* In old tunings (¼-comma meantone, Pythagorean) most fifths and thirds are **sweeter and purer than your piano** — but that purity is bought by dumping all the leftover error into ONE fifth, which then **howls**. Here that fifth (G♯→E♭) is a place on a 3-D ring you walk into on purpose: it flares red, the tiles shear and recoil, and the chord beats ~14 Hz. **Play it:** hit *Begin*, tap tiles to walk fifth-by-fifth, drag to spin the ring, and flip the temperament toggle (12-TET · meantone · Pythagorean) to hear the *same* fifth retune — the sweet thirds appear and the wolf materializes on the spot. Leave it 5s and it walks itself across the wolf, so it's alive at a glance. **Why it's the pick:** the sharpest, most *legible* version of "the wrong note is the point" — the wrong note has a name and a dramatic place — on the DOM/CSS-3D surface the jury keeps asking for, fully verifiable and phone-perfect. It can sound genuinely *wrong*, on purpose, which nothing in the last two weeks could.
