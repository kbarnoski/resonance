- **[/dream/102-kids-echo-song](/dream/102-kids-echo-song)** — "Echo Song" (kids) · *Cycle 120*

  **Why open it**: hand to a child. The bird teaches by example — if they tap the same note four times,
  the bird mirrors it and then offers something different. Gentle, no fail state, zero permissions.
