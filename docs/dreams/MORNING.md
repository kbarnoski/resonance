## New since yesterday

- **[/dream/73-journey-arc-spread](https://getresonance.vercel.app/dream/73-journey-arc-spread)** — Journey Arc Spread
  Five of your published journeys — Cosmic Drift, Mycelium Dream, Sacred Resonance, Abyssal Dive,
  Snowflake — each with its own distinct visual arc. Tab between them; each has its own 6-phase
  structure (Starfield → Nebula → Supernova for Cosmic; Spore → Branching → Canopy for Mycelium; etc.)
  and its own visual vocabulary: star field for Cosmic, particle network lines for Mycelium, rotating
  hexagonal mandala rings for Sacred, horizontal sine-wave bands for Ocean, drifting 6-arm snowflakes
  for Winter. Hit Demo and switch tabs while running — you'll immediately see how different the palettes feel.
  **Why open this**: this is the "spread across your published journeys, not just Ghost" directive made
  visual. It's a map of how differently the same arc engine can feel across different journey themes.
