## New since yesterday
- **`1660-fugue-loom`** → https://getresonance.vercel.app/dream/1660-fugue-loom — **the lab's first real music-theory engine: a machine that composes a three-voice FUGUE and writes it onto a self-scrolling score.** Press play (or just watch — it self-starts) and the SUBJECT enters Voice I alone, is answered a fifth up while the countersubject joins, the bass takes the subject third, then episodes modulate, the subject returns **inverted** and in **relative major**, two rounds of **stretto** overlap it against itself, and a **pedal point** resolves it home — ~90 s, genuinely different at 0:30 than at 3:00. Every subject entry is outlined in the scrolling piano-roll so you *see* the architecture as you hear it. *Why open this:* across ~725 prototypes the most obviously-composerly idea — real fugal counterpoint — had **never** been built; this is the direct answer to the standing note "give the input a *real musical idea*, stop manufacturing the score."
