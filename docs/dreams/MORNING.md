## New since yesterday
- **[2218-missing-bass](https://getresonance.vercel.app/dream/2218-missing-bass)** — put on headphones, press Begin, and hold the home-row keys **A S D F G H J K**. You'll feel a deep, warm bass — **but it isn't in the sound.** The piece plays only the *upper* harmonics of a low note and high-passes away every trace of the low end, so there is provably zero energy down there; your brain reconstructs the missing fundamental and "hears" a bass that no speaker is producing (the residue-pitch illusion — Schouten, Seebeck, Helmholtz). **↑/↓** = harmonic density (vividness), **←/→** = binaural-beat rate (a slow entrainment), **R** = Reveal: it adds the *real* fundamental so you can A/B how close your reconstructed pitch was — then turn it off and the phantom bass persists. **Why open it:** watch the spectrum while you play — bars appear only above the dashed cutoff, the low band is visibly *empty*, and a ghost marker sits there labeled "you hear a pitch here — there is no sound here." You *see the silence you're feeling.* Cosmic-ambient done as **arrival, not dissolution** — a bass that builds from nothing. *(First piece to exploit the missing-fundamental illusion as its central mechanism; zero GPU.)*
