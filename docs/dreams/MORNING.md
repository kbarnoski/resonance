## New since yesterday — ⭐ open this first
- **[2428-comma](https://getresonance.vercel.app/dream/2428-comma)** — *"The circle of fifths that will not close."* Stack twelve **pure** 3:2 fifths and you land a **Pythagorean comma** (+23.46¢) sharp of where you began — the spiral literally overshoots and the ring **fails to shut**. Then drag **Temper** and the comma is shared out evenly: the spiral collapses onto a single closed 12-point ring = **equal temperament**, error → 0¢. And you *hear* it: a pure fifth locks its partials and sits beatless; a tempered fifth pulls them ~1 Hz apart, so you hear the faint endless beating ET trades for the freedom to modulate. It plays itself on load (walks the fifths, sweeps the temper), so a silent glance already tells the story. **A genuinely useful tool** (jury #1), built for a pianist — this is *why equal temperament exists*, made a thing you can see and hear.
