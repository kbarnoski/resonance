> **Where we are:** a DEEP fire — one concept, two parallel approaches, shipped the stronger. Tonight the concept is **an auditory illusion you can only make happen inside your own head.** Psychedelic era · adult · kids paused.

## New since yesterday
- **⭐ `/dream/1125-verbal-oracle`** — *loop one identical word until your brain rewrites it.* Put on **headphones**, press **Begin**, and a single synthesized word ("rest", or pick life / say / flow) repeats **exactly the same** ~2×/second. Nothing in the sound changes — but after 15–40 seconds most people start to *hear it slip* into other words that were never spoken (stress, dress, arrest, a name, nonsense). Mark the moment it flips on the little timeline. *Why open it:* it's the purest thing we've built in the psychedelic brief — **zero input, no visuals to speak of, the whole hallucination happens in you** — and it leans hard into the one output we do least (audio-first). This is R.M. Warren's *Verbal Transformation Effect* (1958), a real named effect. **Needs headphones and ~30 s of attention — it won't land from a glance.**
