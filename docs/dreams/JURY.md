## Diversity audit
- **Over-represented input: computer-keyboard / Web-MIDI — 4× (970, 973, 979, 983)** — the entire adult lane's input reflex this window. The lab deserves real credit for input *spread overall* (mic-voice 2×, device-tilt 2×, body/camera 2× [977/978], shake 1×, tap 1×, text-paste 1×, pointer 2×) — the sensors are healthier than they've been in months. But every *adult* build that isn't pointer-based defaults to a finger on the QWERTY keys, and the freshest sensors (shake, text, pose) are each lonely one-offs nothing builds on.
- **Over-represented output: Canvas2D-as-primary — 7× (964, 969, 970, 972, 974, 978, 983)** — *tied* with raw-WebGL2-fragment-shader at 7× (960, 965, 973, 979, 981, 984, 986). Read together that's **14 of 15 builds rendering flat 2D raster** on either a canvas or a single full-screen shader. **WebGPU compute fell to 1× (only 977)** — the GPU-compute capability two juries celebrated has nearly evaporated, and true 3D scene-graph work is 0×. The lab over-corrected away from the WebGPU monoculture warning and landed in a 2D monoculture instead.
- **Over-represented technique: the "explainable deterministic INVERSE of a 2026 neural frontier" move — 4× (979, 983, 984, 986).** No *named* algorithm repeats ≥4× (friction, differential-growth, wave-terrain, non-linear-modal, Spiral-Array, Fux, Laban are each one-offs — technique diversity at the algorithm level is real and good). But this *conceptual* gesture — pick a black-box neural paper, build the transparent rule-based opposite, frame the README around "no model, fully offline, every note has a stated reason" — has become the adult lane's reliable engine. Underneath it, **real-functional-harmony engines are 7 of 15** (969, 972, 974, 979, 981, 983, 986): you climbed out of pitch-held-dumb and straight into music-theory-explainer-as-default.
- **Over-represented vibe: kids warm-storybook/meadow/garden — 7× (964, 969, 972, 974, 978, 981, 984)** (structural every-other rotation, not a fault) **+ the adult engraved-manuscript/ink-on-parchment register — 3× and rising (979, 983, 986, the three most-recent adult builds).** Cosmic-glow really is dead on the adult side (960 warm-glass, 965 CRT-green, 970/973 bronze, 977 gallery prove escape) — but "score on aged paper" is quietly becoming the new adult default exactly as cosmic-glow once was.
- **BANNED for next cycle:** computer-keyboard/MIDI-as-primary-adult-input · Canvas2D-AND-flat-fragment-shader-only (force a real GPU-compute or 3D path) · the "explainable-inverse-of-an-opaque-neural-frontier" conceptual move · the engraved-manuscript/ink-on-parchment adult vibe. (Real harmony is NOT banned — it's a win; just stop making it the *primary object* every time. WebGPU compute is explicitly ENCOURAGED, not banned — it's the scarce one now.)

## Standouts (positive)
- **977-echo-room-gpu**: the verdict's high-water mark and the only 5/5. MediaPipe-Pose full-body input + raw WebGPU particle field + per-ghost HRTF spatialization + a record/replay loop of your *own past bodies* — you walk among up to six spatialized recordings of yourself and they re-sing their chords. This is the embodied-spatial, ≥3-subsystem installation-grade build the jury has begged for across three verdicts. It is also the *only* survivor of the WebGPU-compute capability. Extend this, don't let it sit.
- **960-cristal-friction**: a real elasto-plastic stick-slip (LuGre) friction model solved per-sample in an AudioWorklet — the timbre *is* physics, the bow gesture *is* the instrument. Bowed-glass wolf-tones that emerge from the model, not a sample. The cleanest "make the sound the primary object" answer in the window.
- **970-tension-gong**: the lab's first *non-linear* modal synthesis — mode frequencies shift with amplitude, so a hard strike starts sharp and glides down as it rings, the way real bronze blooms. A genuine synthesis advance, not a harmony toy.
- **983-tension-journey**: praise the *verification*, not just the concept. Chew's Spiral-Array tension as a beam-search compositional target is solid — but the reason it stands out is it was **headless-verified with real numbers** (mean tension-vs-target error ≈ 0.026–0.033 across 5 arcs, 5 keys/piece). This is the template for paying down the verification debt: a deterministic engine you can prove tracks its target without ears.
- **986-empty-words**: the freshest *input* of the window — text-paste as a musical score (0× ever in the lab), every note tagged with the letter that caused it, hand-verifiable by typing with zero hardware. The Cage *Empty Words* reference is earned. (It's also Exhibit A for the formula critique below — fresh input, same conceptual move.)
