# Concept Jury Verdict — 2026-06-29 (UTC)

## Summary
The psychedelic steer (Karel's 2026-06-28 direction) is producing genuinely
beautiful work — but the lane has hardened, fast, into **seven full-screen
shaders-you-watch in a row** (1038→1050). Six of the last fifteen render on a
WebGL2 fragment shader, six of fifteen are driven by a passive mic (or nothing),
and the most recent five builds are all lean-back fields with no real
interaction. Worse: the lab's *only 5/5* — the Echo Halls thread — was correctly
extended once (1029) and then **abandoned exactly the way 977 was**, the failure
mode I flagged two juries running. The ambition floor still technically holds
(zero 0–1 builds), but the median has sunk: the strong builds are the *older*
half of the window, and the recent psychedelic run is variations on one gesture.

## Diversity audit
- **Over-represented input: mic — 6× (1020, 1033, 1038, 1044, 1047, 1050)**, and
  4 of those are *passive analysis-only* (1038/1044/1047/1050 — the mic just
  ambient-drives a field you don't control). The human↔sound relationship I begged
  you to *vary* last jury has instead **regressed** — these are screensavers, not
  instruments.
- **Over-represented output: WebGL2 full-screen fragment shader — 6× (1038, 1041,
  1042, 1043, 1044, 1047).** Canvas2D is also high at 5× (1024, 1027, 1033, 1035,
  1050) but stays varied. **WebGPU compute collapsed to 2× (1019, 1029) — and
  BOTH are the abandoned Echo Halls pieces.** The scarce capability the *last*
  jury celebrated recovering (4×) is back on life support, and zero of the seven
  psychedelic builds use it.
- **Over-represented technique: "full-screen GPU psychedelic shader field" — 6×.**
  No single *named* algorithm repeats ≥4 (log-polar 1038, raymarch 1041/1042,
  Ganzfeld 1043, hyperbolic-tiling 1044, feedback-trails 1047 are each "lab-first"),
  but they are all the *same shape*: a fragment shader you stare into with a drone
  under it. This is the new monoculture, exactly as "a sim IS the body" was last
  window and "invert a neural paper" was the one before.
- **Over-represented vibe: altered-states / psychedelic — 7× (1038, 1041, 1042,
  1043, 1044, 1047, 1050).** This is the *mandated* theme, so I'm not banning the
  theme — I'm banning the FORM. Pole-balancing (intense↔cosmic-ambient) inside one
  visual form is not diversity. (Kids-storybook 4× — 1015/1020/1027/1033 — is the
  structural every-other rotation, now paused since 1033.)
- **BANNED for next cycle:** passive mic-analysis-as-primary-input · full-screen
  WebGL2 fragment-shader-you-stare-at as primary output · the
  auto-playing-screensaver human↔sound relationship (no real interaction). **NOT
  banned, actively wanted back:** WebGPU compute as a resonating body (down to 2×),
  genuine interactivity, Karel's real Path piano as the carrier wave.

## Pruning candidates (concept-level, NOT for deletion — immutability rule still holds)
- **1041-nde-tunnel / 1043-dreamachine / 1047-tracer-drift (the lean-back trio):**
  each is a lovely field, none is an *instrument*. Input is passive or absent, the
  piece "plays itself," and — being continuous shaders — none can be verified to
  actually evoke the state it's named for. 1041 self-rates 2–3/5. This is the new

- **1044-hyperbolic-bloom:** "first hyperbolic geometry" is real novelty, but

  shader in a row. Novel math, redundant form. The numbering even gives it away: it
  shipped (cycle 597) *after* 1047 (cycle 596), three near-identical fires deep.
- **1050-mycelial-grow:** the closest to escaping the rut — warm pole, Canvas2D not
  a shader, honestly flagged as *not* lab-first (322-kids-voice-garden used space
  colonization). But it's still a passive mic-breath auto-grower. It broke the
  output and the palette; it didn't break the relationship.
