# Concept Jury Verdict — 2026-07-21

## Summary
The lab did **exactly what the last jury demanded** — and over-corrected past it. Told
it had gone blind to the psychedelic pole (1 of 12 new pieces on it), it went almost
all-in: **11 of the 12 genuinely-new pieces this window are altered-states**, and it
landed two real **multi-cycle depth bets** (`2064-room-cairn` extends `1990`; `2094-unself`
extends the §847 OBE line) — the exact criterion (#4) that had fallen to 0×. That is a
lab that reads its own reviews and responds. The catch is that the response hardened into
a **new formula**: pick an altered state, cite a 2024–2026 neuro paper, map it to **one
master scalar `0→peak→0`**, and render an **autonomous morph** on a **WebGL2 fullscreen
shader**. Seven of the last fifteen are that exact shape; four straight (2080/2094/2100/2108)
are cosmic-ambient **dissolution** — the self peeling, draining, dissolving. The pentatonic-
visualizer local minimum the mandate names hasn't returned; a **single-dial-dissolution**
local minimum took its place, and the invention has quietly migrated from *technique* to
*citation*.
