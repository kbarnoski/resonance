# 2108 · Re·map

## The one question

What if ego-dissolution were rendered not as geometry but as the
**desynchronisation of the self's internal map** — a field of ~40 distinct
living territories whose borders diffuse and dissolve into one boundless plasma,
then slowly re-crystallise into a **different** map? You return re-organised,
not restored.

## The neuroscience

Grounded in **Siegel, Nichols, Dosenbach et al., "Psilocybin desynchronizes the
human brain," _Nature_ 2024.** The finding: ego-dissolution corresponds to a
massive **desynchronisation of brain activity across spatial scales** that
_dissolves the distinctions between functional networks_, and the resulting
re-organisation **persists for weeks** — you don't come back to exactly the same
self.

This piece makes that literal. The self is a **parcellation** — a Voronoi-like
territory map. Ego-dissolution is that map **losing its borders**. The return is
the map **re-forming with new borders** in new places.

## How it works

### Visuals — WebGL2 fragment shader (`render.ts`)

The parcellation is computed **per pixel** in a `#version 300 es` fragment
shader on a fullscreen triangle (no vertex buffers, no external textures —
procedural value noise inline). ~40 seed points are passed as uniforms
(`vec2` position + precomputed `vec3` colour). Each pixel:

- finds its nearest seed = its **territory** → crisp Voronoi cells;
- blends toward a distance-weighted **softmin over all seeds**
  (`weight = exp(-dist / temperature)`). The **temperature is driven by
  `coherence`**: tiny when coherent (weight concentrates on the nearest seed =
  hard cells), large when incoherent (all seeds contribute ≈equally = one
  homogeneous field). **This single term is the dissolution mechanism.**
- darkens a thin border where the nearest two seeds are ~equidistant — and that
  border **fades to nothing as coherence → 0** (borders melt);
- is domain-warped by two animated value-noise octaves so it breathes and
  drifts like plasma, more strongly when incoherent;
- unifies toward one violet plasma at the floor.

### The re-map (`arc.ts`)

Seeds are a jittered 8×5 grid (40 territories) generated by **mulberry32** from
an integer key. Each full cycle increments the key, so the next map has **moved
seeds and re-rolled hues**. The morph from old map → new map happens _while the
field is dissolved_ (hidden under the plasma) and completes just as the borders
re-form — so the return reveals a genuinely different map.

### Audio — voice bank (`audio.ts`)

Each territory is a **voice**: 20 sustained voices, each two detuned oscillators
(saw + triangle) → per-voice lowpass → gain → `StereoPanner` (panned by the
territory's screen-x) → master → `DynamicsCompressor` limiter → out. Base
pitches are drawn from a warm **Lydian** set (not a pentatonic) on a slowly
drifting root. As `coherence` drops, every voice glides toward a common centre
pitch and its filter converges → at the floor it is **one boundless pad**. On
the return the voices re-differentiate to a **new** pitch set (re-rolled with the
same integer that moved the seeds). Continuous drone only — **no struck,
plucked, or percussive events.** AudioContext is created only inside the Begin
gesture; full teardown (fade, stop oscillators, close context after the tail) on
unmount.

## The arc (autonomous, ~5 min per cycle)

1. **Bounded** — crisp map, ~40 distinct territories, distinct voices.
2. **Desync** — borders begin to diffuse; voices detune and merge.
3. **Boundless floor** — coherence ≈ 0: no borders, one plasma field, one
   unified drone (held ~60 s). The seeds move here, unseen.
4. **Re-crystallise / Return** — coherence rises back toward ~0.85, but the
   seeds have moved and the hues re-rolled → a **new** map, **new** voices.

Then it loops into the next cycle with a new map. It runs fully from load with
**zero input**.

## Controls

- **↑ / ]** — steer deeper (bias coherence down toward dissolution)
- **↓ / [** — steer shallower (bias toward a bounded map)
- **Space** — let go (hand control back to the autonomous arc)
- **Begin** — arm the audio (visuals are already alive)

Everything works with zero keyboard input.

## Safety

No strobe. `coherence` is slew-limited to ≤0.16/sec and all brightness terms
(warp, plasma breath) run at fractions of a Hz, so no phase change or bass swell
can produce a fast full-frame flash.

## Determinism

No `Math.random()` / `Date.now()` in any render or audio loop. Seed placement
and hue/pitch rolls use mulberry32 keyed to the cycle integer; timing uses
`performance.now()`.

## Honest self-assessment

- **Meets the brief:** the parcellation-dissolution mechanism (per-pixel softmin
  with coherence-driven temperature), the moved/re-rolled re-map, the four-phase
  autonomous arc, the per-territory drone bank gliding to unison and back, the
  keyboard depth-steer, house-style chrome, WebGL2 + Web Audio fallbacks, and
  full teardown are all implemented.
- **Could not verify headlessly:** no GPU/audio in this environment, so I could
  not confirm the shader visually compiles/links on a real driver, that the
  40-seed per-pixel loop performs well at high resolution, or that the audio
  balance (voice gain sum vs. limiter, root-drift range) sounds right. The
  `WEBGL_lose_context` disposal path is also unverified against a live context.
- **Possible tuning:** softmin temperature range, border width, plasma-unify
  amount, and the floor cutoff (1400 Hz) are hand-picked and may want tuning on
  real hardware.
