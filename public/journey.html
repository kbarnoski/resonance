The growth thesis

The unit of design moved. It used to be the artifact — the screen, the flow, the mockup someone else would build. Then it became the product — I learned to build the thing myself, and Resonance shipped to a real installation. Now the unit is the agent: I design the behavior, the constraints, and the review surface, and the system does the building.

That's the rare profile. Most designers can't ship. Most engineers don't have the taste to set an agent's standards. The dream lab is proof I sit in the overlap — and that I can hold a fleet to a bar of quality without touching every output.
